About us:

Created to help Blackfoot people reconnect and help non-Blackfoot people further their understanding of the culture.

We want to make learning about culture more accessible and fun, by Indigenizing board games.

The games are designed to help practice language, learn protocols, and understand cultural aspects.

Currently we are focusing on Blackfoot culture, but we hope to branch out and help other communities in the future.

Our mission:

Our mission at Night Owl Games is to inspire people to learn more about Blackfoot culture.

Believing that learning should be fun and for everyone, we aim to help reconnect displaced Blackfoot people, while encouraging non-Blackfoot.

To grow their understanding of the customs and language.
