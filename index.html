The Winter Count

The Winter Count is based on teachings and common events that happen in a Blackfoot person's life. As you go around the board, you encounter modern day issues, while learning about cultural practices and Blackfoot legends*. Play with friends to learn together and test your Blackfoot knowledge through the Napi Challenges.

A winter count is used by Blackfoot people as a way to document. Using pictographs, the tribe's winter count records important events that occur.

**disclaimer for non-Blackfoot** This was created with the intention for players to learn basic concepts about the Blackfoot culture. This is in no way a guide or to be used as first-hand knowledge. This should be used to help spark conversations with Blackfoot elders and community members. Remember each family has a slightly different way of doing things.

Siksikaipowahsin Trivia

Siksikaipowahsin translates to Blackfoot Language (pronounced sig-sig-ai-bo-wah-sin.) Play as one of the 4 Blackfoot Confederacy members and make your way around the board testing your Blackfoot speaking skills.

The first edition consists of Blackfoot words that are common in beginner classes. Designed to help players learn basic words and get comfortable speaking Blackfoot
